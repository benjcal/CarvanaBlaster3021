Welcome to Carvana Blaster 3021!

"The year is 3021. House Carvana has prospered under the reign of Ernie Garcia XXIII, and his dominion now expands into the border worlds. A new hub opens on the desert planet Phoenix Secundus, where Car Maximus also seeks to expand their regime. It is from this hub you are tasked with a dangerous deivery mission, equipped with a secret new space hauler... Good luck."

Instructions

Click on the game screen to start
Use Arrow Keys to navigate menus and ENTER to select
In game use Arrow Keys to move ship and SPACE to fire!

Tips

Blast your way into the highest score!
But be careful to not blow up the WD-40 since they give
the Hauler health!
